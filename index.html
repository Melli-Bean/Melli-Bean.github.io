Pompology is a new religion focusing on helping you connect with your unconscious mind to help better yourself and understand yourself more as a person.

We worship the Psychopomp, a feminine guide to help us through life. We practice active imagination, a form of meditation, to help us connect with her and with ourselves.

You will be surrounded by supportive people who are looking to find themselves and help others do the same. We are a tight-knit community with the goal of uplifting everybody.
